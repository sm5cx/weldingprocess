CO2 vs Gas Mix Spatter Comparison

Pure CO2 creates significantly more spatter than Ar/CO2 mixtures due to irregular short-circuit transfer and higher oxidation potential. Ar/CO2 mixes provide smoother transfer modes and reduced spatter formation.
