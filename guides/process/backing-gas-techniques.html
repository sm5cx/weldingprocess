Backing Gas Techniques: Root Pass Protection and Setup

Backing gas protects the root pass back side from atmospheric contamination, essential for critical applications where root pass quality affects structural integrity. FORMIER® N5 provides optimal protection through nitrogen shielding with hydrogen cleaning action for bright, clean root beads.

Essential Backing Gas Setup

  1. Gas Selection. Use FORMIER® N5 for stainless steel, pure argon for aluminum, nitrogen for carbon steel non-critical applications.
  2. Flow Rate Control. Set 5-8 L/min through backing strip or purge blocks. Higher flows waste gas without improved protection.
  3. Purge Timing. Start flow 30 seconds before welding, continue 30 seconds after arc extinction for complete protection.
