Pulse Welding Control: Advanced MIG/MAG Parameter Optimization

Pulsed MIG/MAG alternates between high peak current for metal transfer and low background current for arc maintenance, enabling spray transfer characteristics at lower average currents. This provides excellent control for thin materials, positional welding, and applications requiring minimal heat input while maintaining superior penetration.
