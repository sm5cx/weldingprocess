Limitations & Considerations

Consider Alternatives For

  • Thick aluminum sections (>12mm) - consider helium blend for speed
  • Heavy stainless sections (>8mm) - Ar/H₂ blend may provide better penetration
  • Extremely cost-sensitive applications - commercial grade may suffice
  • Outdoor welding - wind protection more critical with lighter gases
  • Very high production rates - specialized gas blends may offer speed advantages

Storage & Handling

  • Store cylinders in clean, dry environment
  • Use regulators compatible with ultra-high purity service
  • Maintain clean gas lines and fittings
  • Protect from contamination during storage and use
  • Follow proper purging procedures when changing cylinders
